# eu-ai-act-compliance-tracker
Analysing AI governance readiness across 60 major European companies against the EU AI Act
🚧 Project in progress — Tableau dashboard coming soon

## Overview
Primary research project analysing 60 major European companies across 5 sectors
against 6 EU AI Act compliance parameters. Data collected March 2026.

## Key finding
Transparency_report is the only differentiating parameter — 70% adoption
vs 100% on all other parameters. Retail sector lowest at 35.7%.
